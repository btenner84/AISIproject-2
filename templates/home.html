What is Alpha3I?

Alpha3I is a web based marketplace intelligence platform that combines our unique research methodology with the AISI tool.

About Our Research Methodology

1. Define Topic - The topic can be a product/market but all projects must specify 1+ companies it is focusing on

2. Define Source Base - This would be deciding where the information will be gathered from.

3. Write Questionnaire - To focus the research, a set of questions is prepared. This is based on existing information like company analyst reports, earnings transcripts, and other relevant news sources.

4. Source Interviews - This step involves reaching out to and interviewing selected sources. It's in this step that the AISI tool plays a pivotal role.

5. Publish results and Insights gained - After all the data has been gathered and processed, the results and insights are made available to the client.

About AISI

AISI is a key tool involved in Alpha3I research methodology. Once your topic is defined, context is provided and questionaire is developed AISI will contact your source base(which is provided via Zoominfo/signalhire/linkedin).
